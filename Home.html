How Natural Disasters Has Affected Us


Floods, cyclones, heat waves and droughts have made Orissa the disaster capital of India.Regular occurrences of flood, drought, cyclones, and heat waves, occasional occurrences of hailstorm, whirlwind, tornado, and landslide and the consequent loss of lives and properties is a constant phenomenon in the state of Odisha.
